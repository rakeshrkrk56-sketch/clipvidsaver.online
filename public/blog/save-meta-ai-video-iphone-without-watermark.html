Why You Can't Save Meta AI Videos Directly on iPhone

Meta AI generates short animated videos — usually 5 seconds — from your text prompts or images. The problem on iPhone is:

  • The "Save Video" button in Meta AI adds a visible watermark
  • The share button only copies a link, not the video file
  • The link expires after some time — so you lose your video
  • iOS Safari cannot directly download video files from Meta AI pages
⚠️

Important: Meta AI video links expire when the chat session ends. Save your videos immediately after generating them!

Saving to Camera Roll on iPhone

By default, downloaded files go to the Files app on iPhone, not Camera Roll. Here's how to move your video to Photos:

1

Open the Files App

Find the downloaded MP4 in Files → Downloads folder.

2

Long Press the File

Press and hold the video file until the menu appears.

3

Tap Share → Save Video

Tap the Share icon → scroll down → tap "Save Video". It will appear in your Camera Roll instantly!
